What is HEYHO?

It all started the day neither of us could decide on what to have for lunch. It can sometimes be the hardest thing to decide on whether you're at work or at home, even if you have all of your options laid out right in front of you. But what happens when you're craving for a cosy, home-cooked meal but can't find any of that nearby? That's what we're here for.
